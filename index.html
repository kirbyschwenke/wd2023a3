About Us

Tucked in the serene Blue Mountains, Lilac Valley Farm Stay, owned by Marina Ye, offers rustic elegance and a peaceful retreat. Set on four scenic acres, our beautifully restored barn house provides a tranquil escape. Enjoy our plunge pool and explore the charming cottage garden. At Lilac Valley, we offer a minimalistic haven where natural beauty meets serenity.
